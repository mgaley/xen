QUESTS:

List of available quests, particularly valuable at lower levels. Quests are listed by approximate area, with unique quests first followed by repeatable quests. To quit a quest in progress, type “-quitquest”.

T1:

Help Juniper

Level 1-4 (?)

T1 / Juniper

150 exp + 1 health potion


Juniper the guard will ask you to interact with several NPCs in T1. First Gergin in the left house, then Gordon in the right house who will give you a flask, then Juniper who will reward you 50 exp, then Enasha in the middle house and finally Juniper again for another 100 exp.

Harsads supplies   (VERIFIED)
1+

T1 / Gordon

3.000 exp + gear

Gordon in T1 will ask you for a favor when approached. Type “Yes” and he will ask you to deliver supplies to Harsad, the merchant in T2. After delivering the supplies you will receive your reward from Harsad.

Ordinary ring   (VERIFIED)

1+

Between T1 and T2 / Yalana

15.000 exp

Kill Jorn in his chamber at the lowest level of the dungeon in T1 graveyard (“1-15 dungeon”). Getting there will require you to use gas or trans either through a spell or an item. After killing Jorn you will receive a ring that can be delivered to Yalana who is standing along the road between T1 and T2.

Although available at any level, this will be near impossible at the start of the game, and you will also be prevented from entering Jorn’s chamber after a certain level. However any character above the cutoff level can simply collect the ring by placing a skeleton or fireball on the entrance teleporter and then -destroy it in front of whoever needs the ring. This will take a lvl 1 character to lvl 7 upon delivery.

Shon tree leaf (VERIFIED)

Level 6+

T1 / Enasha

3.000 exp + 3x white potion

Say “Hello” to Enasha and she will ask for 20 shon tree leaves. To complete the quest say “Hello” to Enasha while carrying 20 shon tree leaves in your inventory. Quest does not need to be started to complete.

Shon tree leaves can be collected from multiple places, but one of the easiest places is north east from Oreseth near the westernmost point of the lake. They can also be found west of Odium, a bit south of the eastern corner of the barbarian camp. If you are strong enough to fight wizards they can be found on the obvious peninsula between T1 and T2. There are also a few less available places, like east of Secern and west of Oreseth near the Great Hall.

Elemental task (Kill 20 stone elementals) [REPEATABLE]

Level <14+

T1 / Rygaron

8.000 exp + gear (ie. magic bag, gear, vis baritus scrolls, white potion)

Click Rygaron and accept his quest to kill stone elementals. The elementals are on the island directly east of T1. They do not drop items, but give occasional “Found items” instead. There are also stone elementals west of Solus and on a stone-ringed island off the eastern coast of the northwestern continent.

This quest works slightly differently than other quests. Once picked up you can continuously kill stone elementals for as long as you want, however you will only be rewarded for each set of 20 you have killed upon returning to Rygaron. To stop the quest you need to use the -quitquest command.

Reditus quest (VERIFIED)

Level 15+

Peninsula north of T2 / Ortox

1x Reditus Amulet, 1x Maximus Amulet

North of T2, past the barbarian camp, there is a house on a peninsula. Say “Hello” to Ortox in the house, followed by “Task” and he will send you to collect some specific supplies from the castle in T3. The supplies are in Corsix’ chamber and two of the storerooms next to it. They are clearly visible from the glow around them. After collecting the supplies, return to Ortox. Say “Hello” followed by “Supplies” and “Yes” to receive your reward. You can also skip straight to the second step of this quest if you retrieve the supplies first.

NOTE: This quest, despite being able to repeat the conversations, is not repeatable.

Corins lost ring (VERIFIED)

Level 16+ / complete “Reditus quest”

Peninsula north of T2 / Corin

4.000 exp

After helping Ortox you can approach his wife in the backyard, who will ask you for help. Say “Yes” and she will tell you to go to the barbarian camp directly south. Walk around the camp while killing until you get a message saying “Camp searched” (unknown exactly what triggers this), and return to Corin. She will now cast a spell on you and send you back to the camp. You can now enter a secret room through the SW corner of the camp. Kill Weevon who guards the room and search the open chests to obtain the ring. Weevon will also drop a 3-propped Dragon Sword, and the open chests will contain various propped gear and/or maximus potions. Return the ring to Corin for your reward.

PALUS:

Help Tren Shiam (VERIFIED)

Level >16

Palus / Tren Shiam

No reward

Tren Shiam is by the water southwest of the town square. Approach him and say “Hello”. If you are of sufficient level he will ask if you “quest against these evil forces?”. ORESETH:

Bosses (1-19 dungeon) (VERIFIED)

Level 1-19

Oreseth / Orksylox

65.000 exp + 4x Maximus Potion, 1x Reditus Amulet, Salutaris Ring

Orksylox will ask you to kill four bosses, Droc, Greesning, Hell Hound Trainer and Hell Hound Master, all found in the “1-19 dungeon” northwest of Oreseth. It’s located south of the midpoint between the minotaur maze and the castle of the bone staff wielding Master Sorcerers. The entrance to the dungeon is a black square surrounded by tiles and skulls on a dirt patch. You need to progress through the four areas of the dungeon, killing a boss in each to proceed. Neither are particularly hard and most of the dungeon can be done invisible, with the exception of the Calaveras in the second section who tend to dispel. Return to Orksylox for your reward.

It is not feasible before at least past lvl 10-12 – even if you survive you are unlikely to do enough damage to kill the bosses. It’s also important to note that trying to enter this dungeon past level 19 will simply kill your character with no possibility of reclaiming your gear, which is now stuck on the square that kills you.

INSURGO:

Mystic Orb

Level ?

Insurgo (“Jail”) / Merchant Slavlor

Mystic Orb

To start the conversation about the Mystic Orb you need to be carrying the item Mage Skull, a “Found item” obtainable from the castle of the bone staff wielding Master Sorcerers north of Oreseth. This item will be very hard to obtain prior to level 40. To complete the quest you need to also supply Slavlor with 2x Diamond, 5x Black Sapphire and 80x Spirit Crystal. Both Diamond and Black Sapphire are possible “Found items” from the graveyard west of Odium at around level 20-30, but both can usually also be purchased from the Oreseth consignment. Spirit Crystals can be farmed from about level 30 to 45 from the Pix Malantis north of Insurgo. However, from level 40 and up they can only be gotten from inside the dungeon. If you miss your chance to farm the crystals they can also be found as fairly rare drops from certain bosses (i.e. Zealots), or you can obtain them from converting Spirit gear with Slavlor. Once you are carrying everything you can complete the quest by letting Slavlor conduct his experiment. As a reward you will receive the very valuable Mystic Orb. This can be sold for a huge sum of money, or you can try to obtain an additional 20 or so orbs through other means to claim a larger price.

Daily Dungeon [DAILY]

Level 1+

North of Oreseth

Boss material, gear (see below)

In a pit north of Oreseth there is a patch of differently coloured dirt. Walk into this to be transported into the daily dungeon. The daily dungeon is a high level dungeon that can only be entered once a day. It consists of four different floors based on the enemy level, 85, 95, 105 and 115. Kill 1000 enemies on either floor to complete the quest and be transported out with your reward. There is also fairly good loot in the daily dungeon, depending on the difficulty. No enemies in the daily dungeon can detect invisible, and all deal physical damage only.

Only the kills on a specific floor counts, so if you progress to a different floor before completing the full 1000 the counter will reset. It is not possible to go back to an easier floor once you have progressed. Normally you start in the bottom right corner of 85, but once you reach level 70 you automatically start in 105.

85 is where you normally start. If you complete the 1000 kills here you will seemingly only receive 2 boss materials.

95 is entered from the top left corner of 85. If you complete the 1000 kills here you may receive gear or boss materials.

105 is entered from the top left corner of 95. If you complete the 1000 kills here you will be rewarded with ???.

115 is entered by going to the top right corner of the northwestern section of 105 and passing through four gates of the stone circle, ending with the northwestern one. There are several ways to achieve this, but one option is to walk it like a figure 8, starting by entering NW, exiting SE, reentering SW, exiting NE and finally passing through the NW gate again to be transported to 115.

Weekly Dungeon [WEEKLY]

Level 1+

Northeast continent

Gear

Central on the northeastern continent there is a portal, just below the long horizontal dungeon that houses Sogam, Baraw and the mystic chests. This portal will take you to the extremely hard weekly dungeon. The dungeon has two identical looking iterations and will send you to one or the other depending on your Wealth. The goal is to make it through several increasingly difficult rooms to touch a chest at the end. It can only be attempted once a week, and the opportunity refreshes on sundays.

Rellems / Chet / Bekren Level 1-<15,

800 exp + gear / 1.200 exp + gear / 2.500 exp + gear

In a cave west of T1, in the southern part of cliffs, there are three increasingly difficult named enemies that offer rewards when killed.

The first named enemy is an imp named Rellems. Fight imps to get there.

The second named enemy is a human rogue named Chet. Go through the clearly telegraphed false wall south of Rellem and casually walk past all the non aggressive rogues.

The third named enemy is a human mage named Bekren. This is significantly more difficult as you need to make your way past quite a few spellcasters to get there.

The dungeon can not be entered while on a quest or after a certain level. It also requires you to complete a quest to re-enter every time you exit for any reason after killing either named enemy. Trying to re-enter before completing a quest sends you to your “mark1”.
