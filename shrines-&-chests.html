Each character has a built in direction on the clock. So if a char stepping on it points to South, it'll always point south. The actual desired direction changes, not based on time, but seemingly based on where it was previously.

Spirit Shrine

The spirit shrine is the small outdoor shrine ne of Oreseth/north of Oreseth East Ogres.

The shrine updates/rerolls the spirit prop set on your character, visible when you type L and read the top line.

Possible recipes:

drop the items on the square, and type -spirit

50g + 1 black sapphire = refresh the remaining days on the current skil.

1p + 1bs = roll new single prop

5p + 10 black sapphire = roll 1-4 spirit properties on character (?)

Trying less than that, seems to provide lower quality results, but some people say even the 50g/1bs is good if you just do it enough, since you can do it 10x more often, even if it does nothing but refresh half the time.
